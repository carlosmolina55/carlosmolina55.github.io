01 / Profile

From energy systems
to data pipelines.

My background is Energy Engineering (Universidad de Sevilla & Universidad de Málaga, spec. Renewable Energies) — power systems, electrical grids, solar, wind and grid integration. That gave me something most data profiles lack: real domain knowledge of how industry and energy actually work.

Now I'm completing a Double Master's in Industrial Engineering & AI at Universidad Loyola. The Industrial Engineering track is the degree that professionally qualifies me as an Industrial Engineer — Spain's most versatile engineering profile, spanning industrial installations & construction, automation, manufacturing systems, thermal & hydraulic installations, structural analysis and chemical process engineering. Energy is my specialty, not my limit.

The AI track layers the modern stack on top: deep learning, advanced computer vision (OpenCV, YOLO, Roboflow) and MLOps — experiment tracking with MLflow and Weights & Biases, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment with AWS SageMaker.

The goal is simple: work where physical infrastructure produces massive data — energy grids, industrial plants, telemetry — and build the systems that turn it into decisions.
